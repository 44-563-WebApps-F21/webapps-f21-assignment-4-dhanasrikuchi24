Lord Windermere

ACT I._Morning-room in Lord Windermere’s house_.

[5:28 PM, 9/22/2021] Mahi Nw:

Enter LADY WINDERMERE, PARKER, and LORD DARLINGTON

LADY WINDERMERE

LADY WINDERMERE _is at table R._, _arranging roses in a blue bowl_.

PARKER

Is your ladyship at home this afternoon

LORD DARLINGTON

How do you do, Lady Windermere?

LADY WINDERMERE

How do you do, Lord Darlington? No, I can’t shake hands with you. My hands are all wet with these roses. Aren’t they lovely? They came up from Selby this morning.

LORD DARLINGTON

They are quite perfect. [_Sees a fan lying on the table_.] And what a wonderful fan! May I look at it?

LADY WINDERMERE

Do. Pretty, isn’t it! It’s got my name on it, and everything. I have only just seen it myself. It’s my husband’s birthday present to me. You know to-day is my birthday?

LORD DARLINGTON

No? Is it really?

LADY WINDERMERE

Yes, I’m of age to-day. Quite an important day in my life, isn’t it? That is why I am giving this party to-night. Do sit down. [_Still arranging flowers_.]

LADY WINDERMERE

It’s best for me to see him before to-night. I’m glad he’s come.

Lady Jedburgh and Mrs. MRS. ERLYNNE

LADY JEDBURGH

Good night, Lady Windermere. What a fascinating woman Mrs. Erlynne is! She is coming to lunch on Thursday, won’t you come too? I expect the Bishop and dear Lady Merton.

MRS. ERLYNNE

Charming ball it has been! Quite reminds me of old days. [_Sits on sofa_.] And I see that there are just as many fools in society as there used to be. So pleased to find that nothing has altered! Except Margaret. She’s grown quite pretty. The last time I saw her—twenty years ago, she was a fright in flannel. Positive fright, I assure you. The dear Duchess! and that sweet Lady Agatha! Just the type of girl I like! Well, really, Windermere, if I am to be the Duchess’s sister-in-law—

[CECIL GRAHAM _comes towards him laughing_.]

Ah, you may laugh, my boy, but it is a great thing to come across a woman who thoroughly understands one.

[_Enter_ LORD AUGUSTUS _R.U.E. carrying bouquet_.]

Dear lady, I am in such suspense! May I not have an answer to my request?
